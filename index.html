As such, GO360 is uniquely positioned to significantly extend internet Broadband services Rural Nigeria and contribute in the critical areas of bridging the digital divide, improving drivers of higher quality of life, and introducing socio-economic growth engines, being high priority of the Federal government of Nigeria and for the benefit of the rural communities themselves and the country at large.

WE ARE HERE

Nigeria is a nation that wants to grow, and a key factor to enable and promote such growth is having an adequate communications infrastructure in the country. Nigeria of 2019-2020 still suffers from low Internet Broadband penetration rates, which are mainly dominant in rural communities. These under-served and underprivileged communities are the biggest sufferers of this reality and require immediate support that will enhance themselves and the country at large.

In light of the above, satellite communication is identified as the most suitable technology to overcome the geographical barrier extending these services to remote areas, nevertheless, it still involves high Capex and Opex investments which act as a barrier for achieving satisfactory penetration. As a result of the above setbacks, the rural areas are being left lagging behind and the digital divide is growing.
Given the above challenges associated with extending communication infrastructure to the rural areas, especially in a geographically large country as Nigeria, there is an immediate need for responsible and patriotic acts to serve additional contribution towards increasing Broadband Internet and ICT penetration in the rural areas and in the country at large.GO360 has been established for that purpose.
